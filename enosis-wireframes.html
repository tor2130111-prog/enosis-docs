First Look

Spotlight Enosis — App Walkthrough

A first look at how Spotlight Enosis works: a simple, modern app for finding people who share your passion — through their real work, not résumés or job listings.

What's in the first version
  • Network — one feed, everyone's Posts, filtered by passion tag + city. This is the app's home screen.
  • Profile — identity + passion tags + your Posts grid. That's the whole profile.
  • Chat — direct 1:1 messaging only.
  • Settings & Safety — account basics, one privacy toggle, delete account, report/block.
On the roadmap for future updates
  • An opportunities board — post and apply to open roles on real passion projects, once the community is thriving.
  • Deeper profiles — skills, credit history, and reputation, added as the community grows. For now, the focus stays on real work and genuine passion.
  • Spotlight pins, an activity feed, and group chats with an AI planning assistant — all planned as the app grows past this first version.
