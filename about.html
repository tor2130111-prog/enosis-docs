About the project

Spotlight Enosis

A new kind of social network for the entertainment and creative world.

Spotlight Enosis is a new social networking app built for helping people in the entertainment industry and creative world find real, local collaborators. This app isn't about finding job listings, casting calls, or talent. It's about finding people who share your passion, discovered through the work they actually enjoy making.

The problem it solves

If you're an actor, artist, musician, comedian, editor, writer, composer, producer, or anyone else with a passion project, your options for finding collaborators are limited and often feel impersonal, which can take away from the effort you really want to put in. Casting platforms treat you like a job applicant. General social media is built for scrolling and entertainment, not for actually connecting with the right person to build something with. Finding the right people for your project can take more time, money, and grief than it should. This app helps you find local people with the same taste, style, and passion that fits what you're trying to make.

How it works

Every person on Spotlight Enosis sets up a simple profile: who they are, what they're passionate about creatively, and optionally a feed of their real work, photos and videos of what they've actually made. Other members discover each other by browsing that work, filtered by location and skills or interest. When you find someone whose work resonates with you, you message them directly.

Who it's for

Anyone with a creative passion, project, or idea who wants the right person or people to build it with. A guitarist wants to find people to make a band with. The band wants to find a local drummer. The local drummer wants to find people to make a music video. Or an artist wants to meet a local animator to develop a friendship. It's not limited to any specific creative field or goal. This is for anyone who wants to find people to learn and create with.

Why it matters for the entertainment industry

Other networks weren't built for this. LinkedIn runs on résumés, job titles, and formal credentials, which makes sense for corporate hiring but has little to do with a passion project, where what matters most is your taste and the work you actually make. Instagram and TikTok keep people scrolling and watching, which is fine for building an audience but does little to help you find the one person you need to build something with.

Spotlight Enosis works differently. You can find collaborators in your own city for in-person work, or connect with people anywhere in the world for remote projects. It's free to use, so anyone with a passion project can take part regardless of budget or industry experience. Messaging is direct and private once you've found someone, with reporting and blocking built in from the start, since real people are meeting to collaborate on real projects. We'll keep adding more protections as the app grows.

Built by youth. Built for all.
